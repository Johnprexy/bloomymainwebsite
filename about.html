About Bloomy Technologies

Transforming careers through world-class technology education since 2021. From our roots in the United States of America to our expansion across Nigeria, we're building the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Journey

From a vision in the USA to empowering thousands across two continents

Founded with Purpose

Bloomy Technologies was founded in 2021 in the United States of America by Engr. Dotun Oketade, a visionary engineer with a passion for bridging the technology skills gap. Recognizing the growing demand for skilled tech professionals, we set out to create a training institute that would not just teach technology, but transform careers.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that with the right training, mentorship, and support, anyone can build a successful career in technology. This vision inspired us to develop comprehensive curricula, partner with industry leaders, and create an ecosystem where students do not just learn but thrive.

In 2024, we expanded our operations to Nigeria, and in 2025, we established our branch in Ikeja, Lagos, bringing world-class tech education closer to the African market. This expansion reflects our commitment to global impact and our belief in the immense potential of African tech talent.
